## Customer Churn Analysis – Project Description
Customer Churn Analysis is a Data Science/Machine Learning project used to identify customers who are likely to stop using a company’s service. Businesses such as telecom, banking, insurance, and subscription platforms use churn analysis to improve customer retention and reduce revenue loss.
## In this project, customer data such as:
Age ,
Gender ,
Monthly charges ,
Contract type ,
Internet service ,
Tenure ,
Payment method ,
Customer support usage ,
is analyzed to predict whether a customer will stay or leave (churn).
## The project includes:
Data Collection & Preprocessing – Cleaning missing values and preparing data ,
Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) – Finding patterns and trends using charts and statistics ,
Feature Engineering – Selecting important variables affecting churn ,
Model Training – Using machine learning algorithms like Logistic Regression, Random Forest, and XGBoost ,
Model Evaluation – Measuring accuracy using metrics such as Accuracy Score, Confusion Matrix, Precision, Recall, and ROC-AUC ,
Prediction System – Predicting churn for new customers .
## Objective
# The main goal is to help companies:
Understand why customers leave ,
Improve customer satisfaction ,
Increase retention rates ,
Make data-driven business decisions .
## Technologies Used
Python ,
Pandas ,
NumPy ,
Matplotlib / Seaborn ,
Scikit-learn ,
Jupyter Notebook .
## Outcome
The system predicts customer churn and highlights the important factors influencing customer behavior, helping businesses take preventive actions to retain customers.
